Legs chicken with rice

Description

This is a very popular meal in mi country, Argentina, generally we eat this on raining days.

Ingredients.

  • 1 cup of warm water
  • flour
  • a tablestoon of fat
  • azucar(optional)
  • a tablestoon of salt
  • oil

Steps

  1. Put the flour in a bowl
  2. Put the the water,salt and fat in the center of bowl with the flour
  3. Mix with your hands until smooth
  4. Put the flour in a bag for 15 minutes
  5. Put the flour in a bag for 15 minutes
  6. Stretch the flour and cut it into portions
  7. Put to boil in oil
  8. And ready!If you want can put azucar on it.
